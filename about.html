About us

Nestled deep within the serene forests of Norway, Rainy Days was born out of a profound appreciation for the beauty that flourishes when raindrops fall. Surrounded by towering pines, hidden lakes, and misty trails, we found inspiration in the magic that rain brings to nature — an invitation to explore the world in all its moods, not just when the sun shines.

Drawing on generations of outdoor experience, we crafted rain gear designed to let you wander through storms, hike beneath the drizzle, and find joy in the gentle patter of raindrops on leaves. Each of our jackets is a tribute to Norway's wilderness — rugged, resilient, and ready for anything.

Rainy Days is more than a brand; it’s a celebration of the landscapes that come alive under gray skies. We invite you to step outside, feel the fresh air, and discover why the forest, the fjords, and the mountains are even more magical in the rain. Let us help you turn rainy days into unforgettable adventures.

How to Measure

  • Chest:

    Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the measuring tape under your arms and parallel to the ground.

  • Waist:

    Measure around the narrowest part of your waist, typically just above the belly button.

  • Sleeve Length:

    Measure from the center of the back of your neck, across your shoulder, and down to your wrist.

NB!

If you plan to wear layers under your jacket, we recommend choosing a slightly larger size for extra comfort. Our jackets are designed with an active fit to allow for easy movement whether you're hiking, cycling, or simply enjoying a rainy day stroll.
